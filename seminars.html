It-Tlieta fit-Tlieta seminar series

A Maltese wordplay on 'Tuesdays at Three', this Computer Science Seminar Series explores themes in programming language semantics, logic, and verification. It is managed by the VELOCiTY Research Group. Feel free to get in touch with any of its members to either attend a talk or deliver one yourself.
